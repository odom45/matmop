## Certification Levels & Curriculum

### Level 1: Foundation (Free)

**Certification Name:** MATMOP Certified Associate (MCA)

**Target Audience:** New freelancers, students, career transitioners

**Prerequisites:** None

**Curriculum Modules:**

**Module 1.1: Introduction to AI-Human Collaboration (2 hours)**
- What is MATMOP and how it works
- Understanding AI agents and their capabilities
- The role of human oversight in AI work
- Ethics and responsible AI use
- Platform navigation and setup

**Module 1.2: Freelance Fundamentals (3 hours)**
- Overview of major freelance platforms
- Understanding job types and categories
- Client communication best practices
- Setting up profiles on Upwork, Freelancer, Fiverr
- Understanding job proposals and bidding

**Module 1.3: Basic Task Oversight (4 hours)**
- What is drift in AI-generated work
- Identifying common AI errors and hallucinations
- Checking work against client requirements
- Basic quality assurance checklist
- When to request agent rework vs manual correction

**Module 1.4: MATMOP Platform Operations (3 hours)**
- Navigating the MATMOP dashboard
- Understanding the job workflow
- Communicating with AI agents
- Submitting work for approval
- Handling client revisions

**Module 1.5: Simple Job Types (4 hours)**
- Content writing oversight
- Data entry verification
- Basic graphic design review
- Simple research task validation
- Social media content approval

**Assessment:**
- 50-question multiple choice exam (70% passing score)
- 2 practical exercises: Review AI-generated content samples
- Estimated study time: 16-20 hours

**Capabilities After Certification:**
- Access to MATMOP platform
- Job types: Basic content, data entry, simple research
- Job value limit: Up to $500 per job
- AI agent access: 1 generalist agent + 3 specialized agents
- 10% creator fee applies

**Renewal:** Free (one-time certification)
